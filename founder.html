    Adeyemi Salau.

    Adeyemi Salau is a graduate of Computer Science from the University of Jos, Plateau State Nigeria. He graduated in 2008. He started coding in 2004. He was the pioneer team member of the University of Job Web team where he was awarded by the University for his contributions helping the University make Webo matrix ranking in 2009. He is currently the founder of Lessonotes a Google Cloud Startups awardee and enrollee, Commitment an escrow financial services startup, AskNigeria, Saddle Bearers Charity Foundation, Brands Nigeria a Data Analytics using consumer sentiment analysis for user reviews, American Court Cases with AI a Legal Research Database for the United States Judicial System, Sophiverse, an app to use Augmented Reality to allow teachers / educators to develop instructional aids when preparing for a class. We would leverage lessonotes user base for this as a pilot phase, Bible Collective a web app with five major Bible versions (KJV, YLT, ASV, BBE and WEB) both in Android and iOS, Jupyter Converter a tool to assist data science and ML individuals to convert Python Jupyter Notebooks into Word and PDF, author USA Jobs a tool for finding Government Jobs in the US, author of Lessonotes Afrikaans Android and Lessonotes Afrikaans iOS Lessonotes Swahili Lessontes Arabic author of Lawyer Jobs in Nigeria a tool for lawyers in Nigeria to get up to date job postings in their locality, OCR to Text Android and OCR to Text iOS, an app to convert Images using Optical Character Recognition to their textual representations and many others just to name a few. He has served in various capacities as a life coach, teacher and mentor and currently a teens teacher in the Redeemed Christian Church of God.
